About

My, name is Brandon O'Regan, I'm a Full Stack Software Engineer who specialises in HTML, CSS & JavaScript.

For years I was a tradesmen. I was tormented by the Australian sun, and had more encounters with snakes, spiders and electricity then any employee ever should.

And in the end, it wasn't the near death experiences that lead me here either. It was something far more subtle that drove me to developing.

It was the realisation that I was no longer learning and growing.

Finding a career that, gave me more energy than it took, combining everything I loved about the electrical industry, like problem solving and team work, and that allows me to continuously learn and grow has been a rejuvinating experience.
